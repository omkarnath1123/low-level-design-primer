The "don't overpay" note

Flagships (opus / gpt-5.5 / pro) are overkill for 80% of tasks. For high-volume, latency-sensitive, or batch work, the nano / mini / flash / haiku tiers usually give you ~85–90% of the quality at a fraction of the cost. Smart move: route cheap models by default, escalate to flagships only when the task needs it.
