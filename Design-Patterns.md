## How to study patterns well
For each pattern:
1. identify the problem it solves
2. identify the abstraction boundary it introduces
3. identify the tradeoff in complexity
4. identify where interviewers commonly overuse it
5. map it to one LLD problem from `problem-statements/`

## Staff-level expectation
Do not just define the pattern.
Be able to explain:
- why this pattern helps here
- why a simpler alternative may be better
- what change in requirements would justify introducing it

That is the difference between pattern memorization and design judgment.
